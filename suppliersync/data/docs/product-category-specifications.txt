PRODUCT CATEGORY SPECIFICATIONS & CLASSIFICATION GUIDE
=======================================================
Last Updated: January 2025

OVERVIEW
--------
This document defines product categories, their specifications, and classification 
rules for the SupplierSync catalog system. Proper categorization ensures accurate 
pricing, governance, and customer experience.

CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
--------------------

1. FURNITURE & HOME DECOR
   Description: Indoor furniture, decorative items, lighting, and home accessories
   Typical SKU Prefix: FURN-
   Margin Range: 15-30%
   Price Volatility: Low to Medium
   MAP Enforcement: Yes
   Seasonal Factors: Q4 high season, Q1 clearance
   
   Subcategories:
   - Living Room Furniture (sofas, chairs, coffee tables)
   - Bedroom Furniture (beds, dressers, nightstands)
   - Dining Room Furniture (tables, chairs, buffets)
   - Home Decor (wall art, vases, candles)
   - Lighting (table lamps, floor lamps, chandeliers)

2. OUTDOOR & PATIO
   Description: Outdoor furniture, patio sets, garden decor, and seasonal items
   Typical SKU Prefix: OUT-
   Margin Range: 20-35%
   Price Volatility: High (seasonal)
   MAP Enforcement: Moderate
   Seasonal Factors: High season (Apr-Sep), Winter discounts (Nov-Feb)
   
   Subcategories:
   - Patio Furniture (sets, individual pieces)
   - Outdoor Dining (tables, chairs, umbrellas)
   - Garden Decor (statues, planters, fountains)
   - Seasonal Items (holiday decorations, summer accessories)

3. ELECTRONICS & APPLIANCES
   Description: Consumer electronics, home appliances, smart home devices
   Typical SKU Prefix: ELEC-
   Margin Range: 10-20%
   Price Volatility: Medium (technology refresh cycles)
   MAP Enforcement: Strict
   Price Protection: 30 days standard
   
   Subcategories:
   - Kitchen Appliances (refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers)
   - Home Electronics (TVs, audio systems, smart devices)
   - Small Appliances (coffee makers, blenders, toasters)
   - Computer Accessories (monitors, keyboards, peripherals)

4. HOME IMPROVEMENT
   Description: Tools, hardware, building materials, and DIY supplies
   Typical SKU Prefix: HOME-
   Margin Range: 15-25%
   Price Volatility: Low
   MAP Enforcement: Moderate
   
   Subcategories:
   - Power Tools (drills, saws, sanders)
   - Hand Tools (screwdrivers, hammers, wrenches)
   - Building Materials (lumber, hardware, fasteners)
   - Safety Equipment (safety glasses, gloves, helmets)

5. TEXTILES & SOFT GOODS
   Description: Bedding, towels, curtains, rugs, and fabric products
   Typical SKU Prefix: TEXT-
   Margin Range: 20-30%
   Price Volatility: Low
   MAP Enforcement: Yes
   
   Subcategories:
   - Bedding (sheets, comforters, pillows)
   - Bath Textiles (towels, bath mats, shower curtains)
   - Window Treatments (curtains, blinds, valances)
   - Rugs & Carpets (area rugs, runners, mats)

RESTRICTED CATEGORIES
---------------------
These categories require special handling and may be blocked from automatic updates:

- Restricted Items: Products requiring special licensing or permits
- High-Risk Products: Items with safety or regulatory concerns
- Age-Restricted: Products requiring age verification
- Special Order: Custom or made-to-order items

CLASSIFICATION RULES
--------------------
1. SKU Naming Convention:
   - Use category prefix (FURN-, OUT-, ELEC-, etc.)
   - Followed by 4-6 digit numeric identifier
   - Example: FURN-12345, OUT-67890

2. Multi-Category Products:
   - Assign to primary category
   - Use secondary category tags for cross-referencing
   - Example: Outdoor dining table = OUTDOOR & PATIO (primary), FURNITURE (secondary)

3. Seasonal Reclassification:
   - Products may shift categories seasonally
   - Example: Outdoor heaters may be OUTDOOR & PATIO in winter, but categorized differently in summer

PRICING IMPLICATIONS BY CATEGORY
---------------------------------
- High-Margin Categories (25%+): Furniture, Outdoor, Textiles
  → More flexibility in pricing, seasonal adjustments allowed
  
- Medium-Margin Categories (15-25%): Home Improvement
  → Standard pricing rules apply
  
- Low-Margin Categories (10-20%): Electronics
  → Strict MAP enforcement, price protection required

GOVERNANCE RULES BY CATEGORY
-----------------------------
- Furniture & Outdoor: Allow daily price changes up to 20%
- Electronics: Daily changes limited to 15% (price protection)
- Textiles: Standard 20% daily limit
- Restricted Categories: No automatic updates allowed

QUALITY ASSURANCE
-----------------
All products must meet category-specific quality standards:
- Furniture: Structural integrity, finish quality, material authenticity
- Electronics: Safety certifications, warranty standards, compatibility
- Outdoor: Weather resistance, durability ratings, material quality
- Textiles: Fabric quality, thread count standards, colorfastness
