SUPPLIER PRICING POLICY & CATALOG GUIDELINES
============================================
Effective Date: January 2025
Version: 2.1

1. PRICING STRUCTURE
-------------------
All suppliers must maintain a minimum 5% margin between wholesale and retail pricing. 
This ensures sustainable operations while remaining competitive in the marketplace.

Wholesale prices should reflect:
- Base cost of goods
- Standard supplier markup (15-25% typical)
- Shipping and handling costs
- Quality assurance overhead

Retail prices must include:
- Wholesale cost
- Minimum 5% margin (governance enforced)
- Competitive market positioning
- Seasonal adjustment factors

2. PRICE CHANGES & VOLATILITY
-----------------------------
Daily price changes are limited to a maximum of 20% to prevent:
- Market manipulation
- Customer confusion
- Inventory valuation issues
- Financial reporting complications

Price change requests exceeding 20% require:
- Executive approval
- 48-hour advance notice
- Business justification documentation
- Impact analysis for affected SKUs

3. CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RULES
--------------------------
Furniture & Home Decor:
- Standard margin: 15-30%
- MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) enforcement: Yes
- Seasonal pricing allowed: Yes (Q4 promotions)

Electronics & Appliances:
- Standard margin: 10-20%
- MAP enforcement: Strict
- Price protection: 30 days
- Warranty considerations included

Outdoor & Patio:
- Standard margin: 20-35%
- Seasonal adjustments: High season premium allowed
- Weather-dependent pricing: Winter discounts permitted

Restricted Categories:
- Items requiring special licensing
- Products with regulatory restrictions
- High-risk categories (as specified in governance)

4. COMPETITIVE PRICING
----------------------
Retail prices must be competitive with:
- Major online retailers
- Brick-and-mortar competitors
- Direct-to-consumer channels
- Marketplace listings

Price monitoring:
- Automated daily price checks
- Weekly competitive analysis reports
- Alert system for significant deviations

5. DISCOUNTS & PROMOTIONS
-------------------------
Promotional pricing guidelines:
- Minimum 7-day notice required
- Maximum discount: 40% off retail
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday: Special rules apply
- Clearance pricing: Requires inventory justification

6. SUPPLIER REQUIREMENTS
------------------------
All suppliers must:
- Maintain accurate pricing data
- Provide advance notice of price changes
- Submit quarterly pricing reviews
- Respond to pricing inquiries within 24 hours

7. GOVERNANCE ENFORCEMENT
-------------------------
Automated governance checks will:
- Reject prices below wholesale cost
- Reject margins below 5%
- Flag daily price changes >20%
- Block restricted category updates
- Enforce MAP pricing where applicable

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
---------------------
Price-related disputes should be escalated to:
- Primary: Pricing Operations Team
- Secondary: Supplier Relations
- Final: Executive Review Board
